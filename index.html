FAQ

Practical details.

What is Put It Back?
An iPhone focus timer. Pick a length, set the phone down, and the app tracks pickups while the timer runs.
How does it know I picked up the phone?
The app requests motion access during active sessions. Gravity and acceleration readings tell it when the phone leaves the table.
What is free and what is paid?
Mom is free. Sergeant and Therapist unlock together in a one-time purchase. No subscription.
What gets tracked?
Session records, pickup counts, completion state, streak info, selected voice pack, and last spoken line — all kept locally. Anonymized product events are sent to Firebase Analytics. Full list on the privacy page.
Privacy

Your sessions stay on your phone.

No account, no cloud sync, no data sold. Motion access is only used while a session is running — the details are on the privacy page.
