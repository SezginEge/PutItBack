Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

This page reflects the current behavior visible in the iOS app code reviewed for this website.

Motion

Used during active focus sessions for pickup detection.

Analytics

Firebase Analytics is present in the current app implementation.

Purchases

Paid voice pack unlocks are handled through Apple's StoreKit and the App Store.

1. Information the app uses

  • Motion data while a focus session is active, so the app can detect pickups.
  • Local session history, including duration, pickup count, selected voice pack, completion state, streak values, and the last spoken line.
  • Preference data such as selected voice pack and preferred session duration.
  • Purchase state related to the one-time unlock for paid voice packs.
  • Usage analytics events for sessions, pickups, paywalls, purchases, and sharing.

2. How the information is used

  • To run focus sessions and detect when the phone is picked up.
  • To restore sessions and keep the remaining time accurate.
  • To show analytics, streaks, and result summaries in the app.
  • To enable and restore paid voice pack access through the App Store.
  • To understand product usage through analytics events.

3. Analytics

The current iOS app includes Firebase Analytics. Based on the reviewed code, Firebase Analytics is used for product events such as app opens, session starts, pickups, paywall views, purchases, and sharing actions.

4. Purchases

Paid voice packs are handled through Apple's StoreKit and the App Store. Payment details are handled by Apple, not entered directly into the app.

5. Local storage

Session history and session recovery details are stored locally on the device so the app can show analytics, remember preferences, and recover active sessions.

6. Your choices

  • You can decline motion access, but pickup detection will not work as intended.
  • You can manage app permissions through iOS Settings.
  • You can manage App Store purchases and restore transactions through Apple services and in-app restore flows where available.

7. Policy updates

This page should be updated if the app's analytics stack, storage behavior, or purchase flow changes.
