Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

This page describes exactly what Put It Back collects, where it sends data, and what stays on your device.

Motion

Accelerometer + gravity data used only during active sessions. Never stored, never transmitted.

Analytics

Anonymized product events via Firebase Analytics. No session text or motion readings are sent.

Purchases

Apple StoreKit handles the one-time unlock. We never see your payment details.

Motion

While a focus session is running, the app reads motion from the phone's sensors to decide whether you've picked it up. Nothing is recorded; nothing leaves the device. Deny the permission in iOS Settings and pickup detection stops — the timer still runs.

What stays on your phone

Your session history, streaks, preferences, and the last line that played live on your device. The app does not sync them to a server. Delete the app to delete the data.

Analytics

The app sends anonymous product events to Firebase Analytics — things like "a session started," "a pickup happened," "the paywall was viewed." That helps us see which features people actually use.

What's in those events: counts, the voice pack name, and session length. What's not: your name, email, Apple ID, the voice line that played, or any raw sensor data. No ads, no IDFA, no cross-app tracking.

Purchases

The voice-pack unlock is a one-time in-app purchase handled by Apple. We never see your payment details. Restore works through Apple. There is no subscription.

Who gets data

Apple (for the purchase) and Google's Firebase (for anonymous analytics). That's it. No advertisers, no data brokers, nothing sold.
